ABOUT OUR APP







Focusing on rural populations and addressing challenges in accident cases makes our product crucial. Forgetting documents during transfers, common among the elderly, can be alleviated with our solution. Additionally, by facilitating data transfer through cards, especially in rural areas, we bridge the urban-rural data divide. The DAO community ensures valid and legal data transfers. In emergencies, instant access to critical health information, like allergies, through our system can be lifesaving, providing essential value in urgent care situations.

Our product not only provides control over medical data but also addresses patient insecurities. By offering complete data ownership and secure blockchain technology, it fills a crucial gap in the market, ensuring patients feel more confident and secure in managing their health information. The Family Relationship Cards further enhance the sense of security by aligning with cultural practices and shared responsibilities.
